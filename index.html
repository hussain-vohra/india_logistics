Customs Clearance

 
Worried if your shipment is held back? IL’s experts are here to give you peace of mind! Our verified and trusted customs service providers are here to help clear your shipment smoothly at origin and destination.
Crucially, customs clearance involves preparing and submitting the documentation required to ship import/export goods from Point A to Point B flawlessly across the globe.
Our licensed experts stay updated with the newest rules and regulations, so your shipment clears the customs smoothly.
If you are worried about the coordination among the custom agents, shipper, transporter and the airline, then book with IL right away. We ensure each supplier work harmoniously with the other, and your cargo reaches right on time.
Our services include:
  • Complete Custom documentation
  • Carting / Receiving goods
  • Examination of the shipment
  • Handling of stuffing & de-stuffing at ports
  • ICD's (Dry Ports) and Customers warehouse
  • Any other customs-related consultation
